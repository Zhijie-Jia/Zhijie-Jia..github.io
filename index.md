### 关于我 About me
主要研究方向为环境经济与能源经济。硕士生导师。主持、参与国家级、省部级课题若干项，获得省级优秀成果奖1项。共发表中文最优/SSCI/SCI等论文30余篇，英文H指数17。中英文累计被引1100余次；累计多篇论文入选ESI高被引论文。代表性成果发表在《经济研究》、Nature子刊、ABS三星期刊（Energy Economics、Technological Forecasting and Social Change等）、SCI检索的TOP期刊（Applied Energy等）上。为Nature Energy, Journal of Environmental Economics and Management, Ecological Economics, Energy Economics, Energy Policy等杂志的审稿人。

His main research interests are environmental and energy economics. He is a Master Tutor. He presided over and participated in several national, provincial and ministerial level projects and won one provincial excellent achievement award. He has published more than 30 papers in Chinese TOP journal, SSCI-indexed journals, and SCI-indexed journals. His H index is 17 and has been cited over 1100 times in English and Chinese journals. Several papers have been selected into ESI highly cited papers. Representative achievements have been published in Economic Research (in Chinese), Nature research journal, ABS Journal (Energy Economics, Technological Forecasting and Social Change, etc.), and top journals indexed by SCI (Applied Energy, etc.). He is a reviewer in many journals, such as Nature Energy, Journal of Environmental Economics and Management, Ecological Economics, Energy Economics, and Applied Energy.
